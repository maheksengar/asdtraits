About ASD

Autism Spectrum Disorder

ASD, acronym for Autistic Spectrum Disorder is a neurodevelopmental disability that can cause significant social, communication and behavioral challenges. Characterised by deficits in communication, social interaction and restricted patterns of behaviour, this is one of the most common childhood disorders. The tendencies of people with ASD to communicate, interact, behave, and learn are different from most other people. Their learning, thinking, and problem-solving abilities can range from gifted to severely challenged.

About ASD Traits Predictor

What this webapp does

ASD Traits predictor uses Machine learning to predict early and significant Autistic traits in toddlers. ASD is one of the most common childhood disorders. The standard diagnostic approaches are cumbersome(requires a direct clinician-to-child observation and takes hours to administer). This increases the waiting time for diagnosis and subsequent delays in access to therapy.

ASD Traits Predictor is a screening application which uses a dataset related to Autism Screening of toddlers to predict the Autistic patterns in toddlers.

The prediction and anticipatory guidance provided by “ASD traits predictor” can streamline the process of diagnosis and help you decide if your child needs to undergo sophisticated diagnostic procedure and other much needed and suitable therapies sooner (which has the highest impact during earlier windows of social development).
